Skills

Creative graphic artist with an eye for detail, whose work emphasizes creativity and innovation to develop unique custom work. Well-versed with most design programs including Figma, Sketch, Adobe XD, InDesign, Photoshop and Illustrator. I have effective time-management and problem-solving skills, allowing the completion of projects with minimal supervision and maximum cooperation with other members within a team.

Ux Research

I investigate competing services and products to understand why users choose them. I assess existing designs to identify how they are being used by users in realtime as opposed to static design documentation. This is accomplished by collaborating closely with engineers to guarantee the new product's successful launch.

Visual Design

Performance driven creative strategist, with experience in creating aesthetic that aligns with the brand's identity while also appealing to the target audience. This involves using appropriate colors, typography, and imagery to create a cohesive visual design. Overall I create an emotional connection with users and guides them towards the product's goals.

Interaction Design

I create Micro-interactions, intuitive Controls and design user flows that guide users through the product's functionality. This involves designing clear and intuitive navigation that helps users move from one screen to another while aiming to improve functionality and create a delightful and engaging user experience.

Usability testing

One of my core strengths is my ability to conduct thorough usability testing to optimize usability and enhance user satisfaction by identifying pain points and areas of confusion. I bring expertise in developing user personas, user stories and journey maps, creating wireframes, prototypes and conducting formal usability case studies.
